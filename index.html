About the IPC Community of Practice & Webinars

The IPC Community of Practice (CoP) strengthens a global network of infection prevention and control practitioners by providing interactive 90-minute webinars, country experience sharing, polls, and panel discussions. The CoP focuses on practical implementation, policy uptake and sustaining IPC achievements.

Target audience includes national, sub-national and facility-level IPC programme staff. Sessions provide peer exchange, capacity building and open discussions among global IPC teams.
