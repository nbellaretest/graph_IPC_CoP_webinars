About the IPC Community of Practice & Webinars

The IPC CoP launched in March 2022 to strengthen a global network of infection prevention and control practitioners by providing interactive 90-minute webinars, country experience sharing, polls and panel discussions. Sessions are hosted in partnership with WHO and regional offices and materials are shared on the online platform. The CoP focuses on practical implementation, policy uptake and sustaining IPC achievements.

Target audience includes national, sub-national and facility-level IPC programme staff. The CoP platform supports peer exchange and encourages capacity building through learning sessions and open discussion.
