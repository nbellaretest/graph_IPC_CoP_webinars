About the IPC Community of Practice

The Infection Prevention and Control (IPC) Community of Practice connects health services staff and stakeholders committed to improving IPC implementation worldwide. Through interactive webinars, live discussions, and shared country experiences, the IPC CoP advances capacity, operational readiness, and sustained improvement of IPC programmes.

Since its launch, the IPC CoP has supported members across AFRO, EMRO and beyond, providing knowledge-sharing opportunities, technical training, and exposure to country-level implementation stories.
