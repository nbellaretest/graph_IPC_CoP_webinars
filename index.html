About the IPC Community of Practice & Webinars

The IPC Community of Practice (CoP) strengthens the global IPC network through regular 90‑minute interactive webinars and an online platform where members share learning, country experiences, and policy implications. Sessions are led by WHO HQ and regional partners and include country presentations, panel discussions, polls and Q&A. Presented materials are shared on the CoP platform.
