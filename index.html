About the Initiative

[cite_start]The Infection Prevention and Control (IPC) Community of Practice (CoP) formally launched on March 8, 2022[cite: 23]. [cite_start]Our focus is to share information, facilitate learning, and provide feedback on issues related to IPC implementation[cite: 18].

The CoP supports individuals participating in IPC programmes at the national, sub-national, or facility level. [cite_start]While we initially launched in the African (AFRO) and Eastern Mediterranean (EMRO) regions, we are actively expanding to the European region[cite: 21].
